# Visual Phishing Detectors (baseline)

We use the following four state-of-the-art visual phishing detectors to evaluate the effectiveness of our attacks.

- VisualPhishNet: Abdelnabi, Sahar, et al. "Visualphishnet: Zero-day phishing website detection by visual similarity." In Proc. of CCS 2020.
- Phishpedia: Lin, Yun, et al. "Phishpedia: A hybrid deep learning based approach to visually identify phishing webpages." In Proc. of USENIX Security 2021.
- PhishIntention: Liu, Ruofan, et al. "Inferring phishing intention via webpage appearance and dynamics: A deep vision based approach." In Proc. of USENIX Security 2022.
- PhishLLM: Liu, Ruofan, et al. "Less defined knowledge and more true alarms: Reference-based phishing detection without a pre-defined reference list." In Proc. of USENIX Security 2024.
